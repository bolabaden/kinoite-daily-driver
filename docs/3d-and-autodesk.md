# 3D content creation and Autodesk

- **Blender** Flatpak is the practical native Linux path for general 3D.
- **Autodesk Maya** has a **Linux** build — licensing and GPU requirements are release-specific; validate against your subscription.
- **3ds Max** is **Windows-only** — use a **Windows VM** (see `virtualization-windows-vm.md`) or keep a Windows workstation.
